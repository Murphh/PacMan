PacMan

Instructions

Welcome to browser based PacMan!

The more coins you eat, the higher your score

You can choose to play the EASY MODE, HARD MODE, or DONALD TRUMP MODE

The harder the difficulty the better your score

If the enemies touch you, it's GAME OVER

If you want to take a break, press the SPACE BAR to pause

UP ARROW to move PacMan up

DOWN ARROW to move PacMan down

RIGHT ARROW to move PacMan right

LEFT ARROW to move PacMan left

View the leaderboard to see how your friends did!

Good Luck!
